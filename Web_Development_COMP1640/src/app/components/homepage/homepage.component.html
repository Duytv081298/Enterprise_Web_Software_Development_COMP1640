ABOUT US

GREENWICH UNIVERSITY

Greenwich University was formed on the basis of a link between the University of Greenwich (United Kingdom) and FPT Education Organization since 2009 with more than 10,000 students from 10 countries around the world who have been studying.

The content of the training, lecturers, and facilities are appraised and recognized for quality by UK experts and Greenwich University..

Graduates will receive a Bachelor's Degree (University Degree) awarded by the University of Greenwich, which is globally valid. With this degree, students can continue to study for Masters, Ph.D. in many countries around the world.

REASONS CHOOSE GREENWICH UNIVERSITY

STUDY IN VIETNAM - RECEIVED BY UK UNIVERSITY

UK education is well-known around the world with rigorous standards to ensure professionalism and pragmatic, to meet the development needs of the job. Studying at the University of Greenwich, students have access to the elite of British education when graduating received a Bachelor's degree from the University of Greenwich.

SPECIAL PROGRAM FOR OJT

OJT (On the job training) is a unique internship program at the University of Greenwich enterprise for final year students. Designed with a total time of 4 months with two training periods, OJT gives students practical knowledge, an opportunity to help students experience careers, gain possible experience for work and jobs after graduation.

OPPORTUNITIES FOR EXPANDING AFTER GRADUATION

Greenwich University graduates are welcome to work at FPT Corporation (with 7 member companies and more than 20,000 employees), and hundreds of business partners and corporations in Vietnam and around the world.

INTERNATIONAL ENVIRONMENT - STUDY WITH INTERNATIONAL LECTURERS AND STUDENTS

Greenwich University strives to provide students with a comprehensive educational environment, bringing professional values. Students studying at the University of Greenwich will study in the same environment as studying abroad with international curriculum, lecturers, and students from many countries around the world.
